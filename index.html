Every six months, leading hardware and software players in the Arm ecosystem come together at Linaro Connect to solve problems, identify issues and plan existing and future engineering collaboration.

For a week, attendees enjoy a mix of industry keynotes, engineering discussions and collaborative hacking sessions. If you are interested in open source software development for Linux, tools and the various vertical segment ecosystems that use Arm processors, then this is the place to be.
