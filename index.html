Linaro Connect Bangkok 2019 Registration is opening soon!

Since 2010, Linaro Connect has been the event to attend if you are interested in open source software development for Linux, tools and the various vertical segment ecosystems that use Arm processors. While the event continues to evolve, one thing stays the same – it revolves around engineering and will consist of a mix of industry keynotes, engineering presentations and discussions, and collaborative engineering hacking sessions. Linaro Connect Vancouver has now ended. The next Linaro Connect will be in Bangkok, Thailand from April 1-5, 2019. Details on registration will be available soon.
